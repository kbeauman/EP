About Open End Tiles

Tiles show Measure and date range in the header, with the selected Open End Questions and Comment Details of each respondent in a separate block. A response may contain up to three Open End Questions, up to five Comment Details, and the date of the response.

Drill-Down Data

Click on a response to view drill-down information on the Open Ends Comment Review sidebar for the individual respondent. Each respondent block is limited to 300 characters for a single comment and 150 for two or three comments. Comments exceeding this limit are collapsed and display an Expand icon (). Clicking the icon displays a dialog box with the complete narrative.
